About Parkour
Brief Introduction

Parkour was established by David Belle in France in 1988.

Parkour is a fashionable extreme sport that is popular all over the world nowadays. In Parkour's world, the practitioner is called "Traceur".

Parkour is designed to get from one point to another in a complex environment, without the need for assistive equipment, and in the fastest and most efficient manner.

Parkour uses the environment of daily life (mostly cities) as the sports venue. People who do this sport use various daily facilities as obstacles or aids, running and jumping through it.
